THE CHASE WEBSITE V2

How to preview:
1. Unzip this folder.
2. Double-click index.html.
3. To share online, drag the unzipped folder into Netlify Drop.

Files:
- index.html: home page with exact horse logo image, eBay link, buy button, pack tracker.
- catalog.html: page 3 / Series Product catalog from your spreadsheet.
- data.js: edit eBay URLs, buy URL, packs sold, and catalog data.
- assets/the-chase-horse-logo.png: exact logo image copied from the latest pack art file.

Important edits before launch:
- In data.js, replace ebayStoreUrl with your eBay store link.
- In data.js, replace buyPacksUrl with your live pack listing link.
- In data.js, update packsSold as packs sell.
